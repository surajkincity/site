Spotable is a digital marketing company specializing in Search engine optimization (SEO). We have a small but talented team with years of experience in SEO, We operate from our office located in Pune, India.

Over the time We have been able to rank our client's websites successfully on google for their target keywords which help them generate new leads. This has been possible because of our dedicated and talented team members which include SEO specialist, full stack developers, and even Google's ex-employees.

We follow ethical practices while optimizing websites, We also specialize in recovering from penalties imposed by google on any website. Weather the penalties are Panda, Penguin or even manual penalties, We have a good track record of fixing all.

Due to our small team size, We are still picking out clients by individually contacting the owners of the websites we like to work with, and not going for mass marketing. We have kept our pricing affordable for the innovative startup companies who really require SEO.

We are always looking for young and talented individuals who are interested in Digital marketing and also hold some basic technical knowledge with a desire to learn new things. If you want to experience how it is like to work with Team Spotable kindly contact us.
